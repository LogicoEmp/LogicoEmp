Most of us in the EA community have come to understand how real the threat of future general artifical intelligence is to our world.

At UC Irvine, we have formed a group of researchers keen to tackle AI alignment problems.

We meet every week. Please contact Neil if you are interested in joining our group.

These are our goals:

  1. To foster a community for people interested in working on AI alignment
  2. To improve our understanding of AI alignment
  3. To share with each other our ongoing AI alignment research
  4. To encourage other talented individuals to work on AI alignment
