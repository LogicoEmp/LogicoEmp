As a club, we meet every Wednesday at 5pm to talk about Effective Altruism. These meeting usually begin with a short Youtube video of an EA talk followed by an informal discussion where we encourage each other to share our thoughts on the topic. We also run activities aimed at getting to know each other better while having fun.

This Fall, we will run our Introductory EA Program.

We're looking for students already familiar with EA to join our Team. Please contact us if you're interested.
